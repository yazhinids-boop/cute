Chapter 1: The Snapchat Twist
It all began with a Snapchat notification. Just a simple friend request. Nothing dramatic—until I opened it. I thought it was one of my schoolmates fooling around, so I played along, asking, “Are you that guy from school?” Turns out, it wasn’t a schoolmate at all. Plot twist—it was a senior from my college whom I had never spoken to in real life! Our chats started casually—memes, quick “hi-hello,” and random late-night convos. But as the chats got longer and funnier, something shifted. The vibe was easy, genuine, and strangely comforting. In a typical desi girl panic move, I played it safe and called him “anna” (brother). Yep, the fastest way to end any romantic hope. Or so I thought. But fate clearly had its own script. Despite the accidental sibling-zoning, our bond grew stronger by the day. We talked about college chaos, annoying professors, and our shared love for junk food. It was never forced. There was laughter, a comfort I couldn’t explain, and a weird excitement every time his name popped up on my screen. Who knew a random social media click could lead to something this beautiful? A snap turned into a spark. A spark slowly lit a fire. But little did I know, this was just the first frame of our filmi love story.
